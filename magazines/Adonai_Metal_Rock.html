About Adonai Metal Rock

Daniel also produces a radio show by the same name, distributed in France.

Issue #1 had Bloodgood on the cover, but is not about the band itself, more, the White Metal scene. The energy of the issue is geared toward explaining and justifying Christian Metal in both musical and religious context.

Only the final issue is published under the name variant Adonai Metal Roxx.
