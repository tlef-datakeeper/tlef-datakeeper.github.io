About Loud and Clear

Loud and Clear is not in any aspect a Christianity-oriented magazine, but demonstrates the energy present in religious / anti-religious expressions of the metal scene at the time.

It happens that the shared Stryper cover is published the same month as the first issue of Heaven's Metal.
