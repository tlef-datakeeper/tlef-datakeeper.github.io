About The Saturday Evening Post

In the late 1970s Saturday Evening Post dedicated one issue annually to coverage of Christianity. Coverage included political figures (Ronald Reagan), televangelists (Robert Schuller, Jim Bakker), and generally well-established Christian music artists.
