About Religious Broadcasting

Religious Broadcasting was a magazine aimed at professionals in Christian television and radio, a specialized equivalent to general industry magazines such as Broadcasting & Cable. Their music coverage is minimal in the 1980s, but expands to include a regular section of reviews in the 1990s, most of which are written by Darlene A Peterson.
