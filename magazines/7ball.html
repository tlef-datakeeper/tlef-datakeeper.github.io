About 7ball

7ball magazine was first published in 1995 by a Thomas Nelson spinoff, Royal Media. Nelson had acquired the Morningstar Radio Network in 1994, and owned Word Records (acquired in 1992). In 1997, following operating losses, Word was sold to Gaylord Entertainment, and the magazine group separated from other interests.
