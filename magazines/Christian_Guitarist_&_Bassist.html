About Christian Guitarist & Bassist

Associated with Guitar Player and Bass Player magazines, Christian Guitarist & Bassist featured tons of gear reviews, plus tips on songwriting, vocalization, production, guitar tabs, and sheet music.

Issue #1, which indicates that this "guide" should be published annually, is the only issue to have been published.
