About Pure Rock

Conceived as a passion project while in high school, Pure Rock Newsletter a tipsheet which evolves toward a professionally-edited 'zine. The final issues are comparable to 'zines such as Adonai Metal Rock, with a focus on presentation and quality interviews. The 'zine received recognition from CCM as a "best of" Christian music fanzine in 1988.
