About The Lutheran

Published until 1962 by the United Lutheran Church In America. At that time the church merged with others, forming the Lutheran Church In America. The publication was "reset" at that time, with numbering scrolled back to volume 1 number 1 in Janurary 1963.

This happens again when the Evangelical Lutheran Church In America is formed in 1988.
