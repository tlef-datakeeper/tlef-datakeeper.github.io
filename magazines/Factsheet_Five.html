About Factsheet Five

Offering primarily reviews of 'zines, Factsheet Five set the pace for similar publications. The publication widely documents the underground 'zine (and perzine) scenes of the late 1980s and early 1990s.

Our capture of their material includes their album reviews, where the artist cited (or generally considered) as Christian-music oriented. For a later publication in similar vein, see Zine Guide.
