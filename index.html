CCM Brasil printed ten regular issues and one special edition, from 1998 to 2000. Published in partnership with a local firm, the magazine contained articles and reviews translated from recent American CCM issues.

News section contents are similar to the US edition, with divisions by style and some entries added about local artists. Overall, the magazine seems to devote more space to American content than local affairs.
